# BerryCatcher

A fun and interactive berry-catching game featuring Pokémon characters!

## Overview

BerryCatcher is a game where players control their favorite Pokémon as they dash around collecting berries. Choose between **Minun** or **Plusle** as your player icon and test your skills with dynamic dash mechanics.

## Features

- **Character Selection**: Choose between Minun or Plusle as your player icon
- **Dash Ability**: Execute powerful dashes by pressing **Space** while holding **Left** or **Right** arrow keys
- **Lingering Hitbox**: The dash ability features a lingering hitbox that allows you to collect berries even after the dash animation completes
- **Berry Collection**: Race against the clock to collect as many berries as possible

## How to Play

1. **Select Your Character**: Choose between Minun or Plusle at the start of the game
2. **Move**: Use the **Left** and **Right** arrow keys to move your character
3. **Dash and Collect**: Press **Space** while holding a direction to dash and collect berries with your lingering hitbox
4. **Score Points**: Collect berries to increase your score

## Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Move Left | Left Arrow Key |
| Move Right | Right Arrow Key |
| Dash | Space + Direction (Left or Right) |

## Game Mechanics

- The dash ability creates a lingering hitbox that extends the range of berry collection
- Combine multiple dashes for advanced berry hunting strategies
- Berries appear dynamically throughout the playing area
